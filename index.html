An AI/ML engineer is a professional responsible for designing, implementing, and maintaining artificial intelligence and machine learning solutions. Their role involves understanding business needs, selecting appropriate algorithms, training models, and deploying them into production environments. AI/ML engineers work with large datasets, develop algorithms, and utilize tools and frameworks such as TensorFlow, PyTorch, and scikit-learn. They collaborate with cross-functional teams, including data scientists, software engineers, and domain experts, to create intelligent systems that automate processes, enhance decision-making, and drive innovation across various industries.
